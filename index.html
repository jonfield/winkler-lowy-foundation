MISSION

Founded by Janine Winkler Lowy and her family, The Winkler Lowy Foundation's primary mission is to support Jewish education, focusing on strengthening existing programs and developing new initiatives to address the evolving needs of the Jewish community. The foundation also supports efforts that foster meaningful connections with Israel and enhance educational opportunities for individuals facing financial or systemic barriers.

INITIATIVES

Jewish Schools

The Winkler Lowy Foundation supports Jewish schools and professionals by ensuring that families committed to Jewish education, who are also shaping it through their professional lives, receive the support and access needed for their children to fully benefit from a meaningful and enriching Jewish educational journey.

Jewish Summer Camps

Keeping kids in the bunk!
The Winkler Lowy Foundation recognizes the lasting impact of Jewish summer camp on personal growth, community building, and Jewish identity. Through its support of the LA Federation's Camp Initiative, the foundation helps teens have continued opportunities to return to camp beyond 8th grade when many drop off. By providing resources and support throughout high school, the foundation helps maintain these important connections, allowing young people to keep benefiting from the unique experience and values that Jewish summer camp offers.

Israel

The Winkler Lowy Foundation is dedicated to expanding career opportunities in Israel for individuals facing barriers to access. Through its dedication to higher education and career development programs, the foundation supports pathways for individuals from historically underrepresented backgrounds such as women and new immigrants, ensuring that financial limitations do not hinder their ability to thrive in the professional world after the army.
