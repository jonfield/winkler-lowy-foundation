MISSION

The Winkler Lowy Foundation is deeply committed to supporting Jewish education, community building, and fostering meaningful connections to Israel. Our mission is to strengthen Jewish identity, promote learning, and create opportunities for young people to engage with their heritage in transformative ways. The foundation also supports initiatives that build bridges between communities and advance Jewish values through education, culture, and spiritual growth.

INITIATIVES

Jewish Schools

Supporting Jewish day schools through educational resources and financial assistance. We believe in strengthening Jewish education at all levels, providing students with the tools they need to succeed academically while deepening their connection to Jewish values, traditions, and community. Our support includes teacher training, curriculum development, and scholarship programs that make quality Jewish education accessible to families.

Jewish Summer Camps

Funding Jewish summer camps to create lasting memories and connections for young Jews. Summer camps provide an unparalleled opportunity for youth to build friendships, develop leadership skills, and engage with Jewish life in a fun, supportive environment. Our funding helps ensure that cost is never a barrier to participation, enabling children and teens from diverse backgrounds to experience transformative summers filled with Jewish learning, community, and joy.

Israel

Facilitating trips and programs that strengthen the connection between young Jews and Israel. Through immersive experiences, educational programs, and meaningful encounters with Israeli culture and history, we help young people develop a deeper understanding of their heritage and the modern State of Israel. These programs foster lasting relationships with the land, its people, and provide participants with tools to become informed advocates and engaged members of the global Jewish community.
